Sonic The Hedgehog

Sonic Generations is a great game that celebrates the history of the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise. Half of the levels within the game are "2-D" levels based on more classic games, and the other half are "3-D" levels based on more modern levels. It is pretty enjoyable to play, and has a lot references to the franchise as a whole including the songs that you can listen to in the record room and (only in the original version) you can play the original Sonic the Hedgehog game.

Release Date

The Game was released in November 2011 worldwide. A couple of months after the 20th anniversary of the franchise, but was still able to come out the same year and be a good game.

Shadow Generations

"Earlier, I mentioned an "original" version of Sonic Generations. Well, recently there was a re-release of the game that included graphics that were improved a little (not by much but still looks better than the original) and more importantly, a new Shadow game along with it. Shadow Generations, as it is called, continues the storyline from Shadow the Hedegehog and is pretty fun to play.
